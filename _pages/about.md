Research Interests
======
My research sits at the intersection of **computational neuroscience** and **machine learning**, with a focus on developing geometric and dynamical methods for learning on manifolds.

My core research revolves around combining **flow/diffusion/bridge methods** with **Riemannian geometry**, **stochastic optimal control** to study general manifolds. I am particularly interested in:

- **Generative models on manifolds**: Flow matching and score-based diffusion models with geometric constraints, like geodesic computation in learned latent spaces.
- **Computational neuroscience**: Trajectory inference across lifespan, and large-scale neuroimaging data analysis.
- **Geometric deep learning**: Leveraging Riemannian geometry and optimal control theory for structured data.
